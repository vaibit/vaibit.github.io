About Me

Born in Derby, raised in Thurles, and now based in Cork. After completing my degree in Visual Communication, my design journey has taken me from in-house teams to freelancing and agency work. With each project, I aim to create thoughtful, visually engaging solutions that connect with people.

Outside the design world, you’ll likely find me immersed in creative side projects, working out, catching up with friends and family, or diving into new music. Travel is another big passion of mine—I recently returned from a memorable backpacking trip through Jordan and around Asia.
